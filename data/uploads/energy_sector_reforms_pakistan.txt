# Report on Energy Sector Reforms and Tariff Adjustments in Pakistan (Q3 2024)

The National Electric Power Regulatory Authority (NEPRA) has announced a revised uniform tariff for the industrial sector to address the circular debt crisis.

## Policy Changes:
- **Base Tariff Increase**: The average base tariff for industrial B3 and B4 consumers has been increased by PKR 5.72 per unit.
- **Fixed Charges**: Introduction of fixed monthly charges based on sanctioned load even if the consumption is zero.
- **Solar Net Metering**: Rumors of shifting from net metering to net billing (at a lower buy-back rate) have been confirmed as under consideration by the Power Division.

## Impact on Manufacturing:
The textile and cement sectors are expected to see an 8-10% rise in production costs. Small to medium enterprises (SMEs) without captive power generation are most at risk.
The uncertainty regarding solar net-metering buy-back rates is expected to slow down the adoption of commercial solar projects in urban hubs like Karachi and Lahore.
